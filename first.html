Maggie Recipe

  1. First boil maggie
  2. Cut the vegetables
    • Onion
    • Green chilli
    • Ginger
    • garlic
    • Capcicum
    • Carrot
    • Onion springs etc...
  3. Take a pan and add one table spoon of olivev oil
  4. when the oil is hot, add onion to it.
  5. When the onion turns a little brown, add garlic and ginger paste to it.
  6. After roasting well, add all the vegetables and cook them lightly.
  7. Then add maggie masala and a little salt to it.
  8. Now add boiled maggieto it and mix well.
  9. Now our tasty maggie is ready.
